### rate_gap/
- **Description:** Intermediate datasets used to construct the monthly mortgage rate gap, including:
  - HMDA-based monthly outstanding mortgage rates (CBSA-by-month)
  - monthly PMMS 30Y FRM series
  - the merged CBSA-by-month rate gap panel
- **Generated by:**
  - HMDA monthly outstanding rate: `code/ch02/02_rate_gap/01_outstanding_rate_monthly.R`
  - PMMS merge + rate gap + national plot: `code/ch02/02_rate_gap/02_rate_gap_monthly.R`
- **Output files (not version controlled):**
  - `msa_outstanding_rate_monthly.csv`
  - `pmms_30y_monthly.csv`
  - `rate_gap_monthly.csv`
